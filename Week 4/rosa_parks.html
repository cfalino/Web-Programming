Rosa Parks

Biography
  • February 4. 1913 - October 24, 2005
  • Refused to surrender her bus seat to a white passenger on a segregated bus in Montgomery, Alabama on December 1, 1955
  • Charged with violation of Chapter 6, Section 11 of Montgomery City Code
  • Sparked the Montgomery Bus Boycott, a movement led by Dr. Martin Luther King Jr., which lasted 381 days




Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. was the leader of the Civil Rights Movement. His famous speech "I Have a Dream" became the anthem of the movement.
